# Core Feature Definition

## 1. User Management

*   **User Registration and Authentication:** Secure sign-up and login for both freelancers and clients.
*   **User Profiles:** Detailed profiles for freelancers to showcase their skills, experience, and portfolio. Client profiles will display their job history and reviews.
*   **Dashboard:** A personalized dashboard for users to manage their projects, proposals, and account settings.

## 2. Job and Project Management

*   **Job Posting:** An intuitive form for clients to post job descriptions, budget, and required skills.
*   **Job Search and Filtering:** Advanced search and filtering options for freelancers to find relevant projects.
*   **Proposal System:** A system for freelancers to submit proposals for jobs they are interested in.
*   **Project Workspace:** A dedicated workspace for each project where clients and freelancers can collaborate, share files, and track progress.

## 3. Communication

*   **Real-Time Messaging:** A built-in messaging system for seamless communication between users.

## 4. Payments and Finances

*   **Secure Payment Integration:** A secure payment gateway to handle all financial transactions.
*   **Escrow System:** An escrow system to ensure that funds are only released when the client is satisfied with the work.
*   **Transaction History:** A detailed transaction history for users to track their earnings and payments.

## 5. Reviews and Ratings

*   **Two-Way Review System:** A system for both clients and freelancers to rate and review each other after a project is completed.
